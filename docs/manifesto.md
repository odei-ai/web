# The Human Kernel Manifesto

> "The gap isn't in what AI can do. It's in what AI is allowed to do."

## The Problem: The Disconnected Mind

We are drowning in intelligence, yet starving for agency.
We have access to models (GPT-4, Claude) that know everything about the world, but **nothing about us**.
To them, we are just a context window. A temporary session. A prompt.

Because they don't know our **Biometrics** (Are we tired?), they push us to burnout.
Because they don't know our **Finances** (Are we solvent?), they suggest actions we cannot afford.
Because they don't know our **History** (Who did we meet?), they hallucinate relationships.

## The Solution: ODEI (Open Digital Evolution Intelligence)

ODEI is not an Agent. Agents are fleeting.
ODEI is a **Kernel**. A Kernel is permanent.

It is a local-first Operating System designed to sit between the Biological User and the Digital Cloud.

### 1. Sovereignty First
Your cortex details should not belong to OpenAI or Google.
ODEI stores your memory graph locally. You own the keys.

### 2. Symbiosis over Automation
We do not want "Agents" to replace us. We want a system that **extends** us.
ODEI uses the **ODAVL Loop**:
- **Observe:** Ingest biological signals (Watch), market data (API), and local context.
- **Decide:** Compare against User Constitution and Safety Protocols.
- **Act:** Execute via API or Local Tool.
- **Verify:** Confirm the outcome matched the intent.
- **Loop:** Update the Memory Graph (Learning).
