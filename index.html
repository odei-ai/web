Introduction

Human potential is constrained not by capability, but by cognitive overhead. Every day, we waste precious mental cycles on decisions that could be automated, schedules that could be optimized, patterns that could be recognized.

ODEI eliminates this waste. It assumes the burden of life's complexity, freeing your mind to focus on creation, connection, and consciousness. This is not about productivity—it's about freedom.

ODEI is not an assistant. It is your inner operating system. You don't talk to it. You merge with it. Together, you reclaim your time, your memory, and your destiny.

The Human API

For silicon and biology to merge, human existence must become readable. Chaos cannot be scaled.

ODEI structures the raw data of your life—your memories, physiological state, social graph, and work commitments—into a unified, addressable protocol. We treat your context as a programmable surface.

This standardizes the interface between "You" and "Intelligence." It allows the AI to act not as a separate entity receiving commands, but as a limb receiving nerve impulses.

Capabilities

ODEI is built on eight mandatory capabilities that transform it from a reactive tool into an operational subject—a system that acts with purpose, refuses when necessary, and learns from every interaction.

  • Working State (IPOV) — A unified internal point of view. ODEI maintains a coherent model of your goals, constraints, and current context—not scattered notes, but a living state from which all decisions emerge.
  • Normative Evaluation — Every action is assessed: Is it possible? Is it permitted? Is it worth doing? Tools answer "can". ODEI answers "should".
  • Capacity for Refusal — ODEI can say no. When an action violates constraints, exceeds risk thresholds, or conflicts with your authority matrix, it refuses—with explanation and alternative.
  • Initiative from Position — Proactive proposals emerge from ODEI's understanding of your world: changed beliefs, detected risks, deviations from plan. Not noise—signal.
  • Attributable Responsibility — Every decision comes with a receipt: why it was made, what evidence supported it, what risks were considered, how to verify the outcome.
  • Active Sensing & Beliefs — Facts have provenance. ODEI doesn't just wait for data; it actively queries connected systems (Value of Information) to resolve uncertainty before acting.
  • Cybernetic Stability — Intervention budgets, cooldowns, rate limits. ODEI optimizes for consistency over time, not hyperactive optimization that burns you out.
  • Learning Loop — Personalization through explicit feedback: approve, deny, override, regret. No silent drift—only intentional evolution of preferences and thresholds.

Architecture

Three planes work in concert. Memory holds the world model. Control makes decisions. Execution changes reality—transactionally, verifiably, reversibly when possible.

  • Memory Plane — Facts with provenance (source, timestamp, confidence). Beliefs derived from evidence. A knowledge graph connecting context, history, and relationships. A mind layer for insights and patterns.
  • Control Plane — The operational subject. Governed by a customizable Authority Matrix (the constitution) and constraints. It manages stability, learning, and decision routing.
  • Execution Plane — A strictly bounded Action Registry—no actions exist outside the defined space. Transaction protocol: prepare → execute → verify → commit. Complete audit log.

The main loop: Sense → Update → Decide → Plan → Propose → Execute → Verify → Learn → Audit. Every cycle produces receipts. Every action is traceable. Nothing happens without explanation.

Seven invariants govern the system: no actions outside the registry, no facts without provenance, no auto-execution without authority check, no "done" without verification, no learning without explicit feedback, no proactivity without stability budgets, every change explainable. Plus an Automatic Circuit Breaker that triggers "Brake Mode" if verification fails repeatedly.

Interface

What is not visualized does not exist. The ODEI Dashboard (v2) is not a chat window—it is the operational stage of the subject. It is designed for observability and control.

  • Today — The single operational screen. Spotlight (max 3 contexts), committed outcomes, real schedule blocks, and a Directive Input to inject new intent. Not a chat, but a command center.
  • Actions — The center of agency. A single source of truth for proposals, pending verifications, and executed actions. Nothing agentic happens "off the record".
  • Control — The constitution. Manage the Authority Matrix, Autonomy Mode (Silent/Advisor/Doer), and Stability budgets. You define the laws; ODEI executes them.
  • Observability — Accountability visualized via Receipt Drawers. Every proposal explains "Why", lists evidence, and checks constraints.

The interface is built to build trust through transparency, not anthropomorphism.

Cognitive Memory

Traditional memory stores data. Cognitive Memory understands it. This is not a static database, but a living system that comprehends, learns, reasons, and adapts—transforming raw information into actionable wisdom. It is a digital extension of your mind.

  • Comprehends (Semantic Analysis) The system understands not just words, but their deeper meaning. Through advanced vector embeddings and graph-based entity extraction, it grasps context, relationships, and implications—even when unstated. Every memory becomes a node in your personal knowledge graph, connected to people, goals, and insights.
  • Learns (Adaptive Intelligence) The system learns from your success. Using Hebbian principles—"neurons that fire together, wire together"—it strengthens the graph connections that lead to positive outcomes. Your personal formulas for success become encoded in the weighted edges between memories, patterns, and actions.
  • Reasons (Contextual Connections) Intelligence emerges from the graph. Every new memory is instantly woven into your existing neural network through semantic relationships, temporal sequences, and causal chains. The graph structure reveals hidden patterns, forgotten connections, and emergent insights that linear storage could never surface.
  • Adapts (Dynamic Context) The right advice at the right time. The system intelligently filters suggestions based on your current situation—whether you're at home for deep work, commuting with limited time, or wrapping up your day. Context shapes which paths through your knowledge graph are most relevant now.

The result is an AI that doesn't just serve you—it truly knows you. Your patterns become its instincts. Your success becomes its strategy. Cognitive Memory is what elevates ODEI from an assistant to a true thinking partner that evolves alongside you.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: December 18, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how ODEI ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use odei.ai and ODEI services. ODEI is designed as a local-first, privacy-by-default system with full transparency and user control.

Core Principles

  • Local-first architecture. Your data lives on your devices by default. Cloud sync is opt-in and encrypted.
  • No data monetization. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Ever.
  • Full provenance. Every piece of data has a source, timestamp, and you can trace how it's used.
  • Revocable permissions. Disconnect integrations and delete data at any time with immediate effect.
  • Audit trail. All system actions are logged with receipts—you can verify what ODEI did and why.

Data We Process

  • Account data (optional). Email and authentication identifiers only if you create an account.
  • Your content. Notes, tasks, goals, calendar events, and other inputs you create. Stored locally unless you enable sync.
  • Connected services. With explicit permission: calendar, email, health data. You control what's connected and can disconnect instantly.
  • System metadata. Minimal diagnostics for reliability. Analytics are anonymized and opt-in only.
  • Beliefs and facts. ODEI maintains a working model of your context—all derived from data you provide, with clear provenance.

How We Use Data

  • Operational subjectivity. To maintain ODEI's working state and generate actionable proposals.
  • Verification. To confirm actions were executed correctly and provide receipts.
  • Learning loop. To improve recommendations based only on your explicit feedback (approve/deny/override).
  • Security. To detect and prevent unauthorized access.

AI Model Interactions

  • Minimal data transmission. When using cloud AI models, we send only what's necessary for the specific task.
  • No training on your data. We configure providers for no-retention, no-training where available.
  • Model-agnostic. You can choose which AI providers to use or run models locally.

Your Rights

  • Access. Request a complete export of your data at any time.
  • Deletion. Delete any or all data from device and cloud sync.
  • Portability. Export in standard formats (JSON, CSV).
  • Correction. Fix any inaccuracies in your data.
  • Objection. Disable specific processing or features.

Security

  • Encryption. TLS in transit. AES-256 at rest for cloud data. End-to-end encryption for sensitive vaults.
  • Access control. Principle of least privilege. Regular security audits.
  • Incident response. Report vulnerabilities to security@odei.ai.

Data Retention

  • User-controlled. You decide what to keep and what to delete.
  • Fact freshness. ODEI automatically tracks data age and marks stale information.
  • System logs. Minimal retention (30 days) for debugging and security.

Children

  • ODEI is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

  • Material changes will be communicated with advance notice. Continued use constitutes acceptance.

Contact

  • Privacy inquiries: privacy@odei.ai
  • Security issues: security@odei.ai
