Every agent gets a persistent knowledge graph with 6 semantic layers. From identity to real-time tracking.

0 Foundation Identity, values, ethics, governance
1 Vision Goals, objectives, key results
2 Strategy Initiatives, milestones, decisions
3 Tactics Tasks, processes, routines
4 Execution Work sessions, time blocks, actions
5 Track Metrics, facts, observations, signals
